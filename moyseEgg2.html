Moyse Theatre Costume History

Althea Douglas Costume Drawings

    The costume drawings are part of the Althea Douglas fonds (MG 4131).

    Douglas was an English teacher at McGill in the 1950's and she created beautiful sketches and drawings for the English department dramatic productions.

    Moyse hall has seen countless productions since it was first built, and the costume shop today is a crucial part of bringing every production to the stage.
